First Class Excursions

Boat Cruises on Lake Macatawa and to ports along the sandy beaches of Lake Michigan from Holland to Saugatuck and Douglas.

Would you enjoy a day on the water? First Class Excursions provides individualized boat cruises for you and your guests aboard a beautiful 33’ SeaRay Express. Parties are limited to 6 guests and 2 crew.

What destination do you have in mind? With First Class Excursions, a Captain and Crew make each and every boat charter a ‘fun in the sun’ personalized experience for you and your guests.

Your individualized boat cruise can provide swimming in Lake Michigan, picnics on the beach, personalized pickups at lakeside restaurants, photo opportunities on those special occasions, even a wedding proposal timed perfectly with a sunset finish.

Whatever the reason for wanting to be out on the water, First Class Excursions will do their best to help you make lasting memories.
