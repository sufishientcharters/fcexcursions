Excursion Ideas

  • Business Meetings

    Board Meetings, Strategic Planning Sessions, Team Building & Employee Incentives

  • Client Entertaining

    A fantastic opportunity to get quality one on one time with clients while showcasing the wonders of Western Michigan, the golfing alternative.

  • Sight Seeing / Sunset Cruise

    Day or evening cruises on the waters of Lake Macatawa and Lake Michigan, don't forget your camera!

  • Wine and Cheese Tasting Cruise

    Enjoy a beautiful afternoon or sunset cruise while sampling Michigan wines paired with the perfect domestic and imported cheeses. *Additional cost per/person

  • Engagements & Anniversaries

    Create a special memory with loved ones that won't be forgotten. From a proposal to a 50th anniversary and beyond, we will help you celebrate that special occasion.

  • Bachelor Party

    How about a pub crawl, arriving by boat rather than a limo or while the ladies are getting dolled up for the wedding treat your groomsmen to a stress free morning out on the water, way better than a monogrammed gym bag or flask. *Limited to 6 passengers.

  • Bachelorette Party

    Make a big splash for the bride-to-be with a night she'll never forget; cruise to Saugatuck Michigan, travel from bar to bar and dance the night away. Limousine service can also be arranged to and from the marina. *Limited to 6 passengers

  • Weddings

    As an ordained minister, I can perform the most intimate of weddings in a spectacular setting. Head out on the waters of Lake Michigan with your photographer and get some of the most majestic pictures, then arrive in style to your reception (Holland and Saugatuck only).

  • Birthdays

    A special treat for young and old alike; Sweet 16 to Fabulous 50 and beyond.

  • Theme Excursions

    Kick you charter up a notch, add a theme like Caribbean with Jimmy Buffet or Captain Jack Sparrow & his Pirates, Survivor, Hawaiian Luau, Western, Fiesta, Musical Decade such as 50's 60's or 70's, Hollywood Stars.

  • Power Boat Handling Instruction

    New boat, larger boat, emergency handling, or just tired of the docking embarrassment we all go through.
